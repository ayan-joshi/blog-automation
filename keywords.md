## Rules for Every Blog Post

1. **Before writing** — identify which green + yellow keywords are relevant to this specific post
2. **Green keywords** — place in: H1 (or very close), intro (first 100 words), at least one H2, FAQ answer, conclusion
3. **Yellow keywords** — place in: body paragraphs, H2s, one FAQ answer
4. **Supporting keywords** — sprinkle naturally; don't force
5. **Never keyword-stuff** — each keyword should read naturally in context
6. **India-specific angle** — always write for Indian parents; mention Indian context, IAP, BIS, Made in India where relevant
7. **FAQ block** — every post needs 4–5 FAQ questions; green keywords must appear in at least 2 FAQ answers
