Features to Consider When Choosing a Cloth Book

When choosing a cloth book for your newborn, there are several features to consider. The material used should be safe and durable, such as 100% cotton. The images should be high contrast, with black and white patterns that stimulate the baby's vision. Additionally, the book should be designed with safety in mind, with no small parts or choking hazards. The Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) certification is also essential, ensuring that the product meets the required safety standards.
