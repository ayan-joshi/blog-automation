Can I use a high contrast cloth book as a tummy time book?

Yes, high contrast cloth books can be used as tummy time books. They provide a stimulating and engaging way to encourage your newborn to lift their head and shoulders during tummy time, which can help strengthen their neck and back muscles.

Yes, high contrast cloth books that are made from safe and non-toxic materials, such as 100% cotton or food-grade silicone, are safe for your newborn to mouth. However, always ensure to check the product's safety certification and follow the manufacturer's guidelines.
