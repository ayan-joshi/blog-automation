Introduction to High Contrast Cloth Books

High contrast cloth books are designed to provide newborns with a stimulating visual experience. These books typically feature black and white images or high contrast colors, which help to develop a newborn's visual cortex. The World Health Organization (WHO) recommends that newborns be exposed to high contrast images to aid in their visual development. Infant cloth books are an excellent way to provide this stimulation, and they are also soft and safe for newborns to touch and mouth.

High contrast cloth books offer numerous benefits for newborns. They provide a stimulating visual experience, which aids in the development of a newborn's visual cortex.
