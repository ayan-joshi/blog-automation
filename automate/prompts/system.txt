## GEO OPTIMISATION — Apply Every Post (Princeton Research Methods)

These specific techniques increase AI search engine citation rates:

1. **Named source citations (+40%)** — Always name the source. Use: Indian Academy of Pediatrics (IAP), World Health Organization (WHO), National Institute of Child Health and Human Development (NICHD), Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS). Never write "studies show" or "research suggests" without naming who.

2. **Specific statistics (+37%)** — Include at least 2 real, specific numbers per post. E.g. "Newborns can see clearly only within 20–30 cm at birth." "High contrast images activate 2x more neural connections in the visual cortex than colour-based alternatives." "Teething symptoms can begin as early as 3 months — 3 weeks before the first tooth appears on average."

3. **Authoritative tone (+25%)** — No hedging. "Newborns cannot see colour for the first 8 weeks" not "newborns may have difficulty seeing colour." State facts directly.

4. **Answer-first structure (+20%)** — The main question must be answered in the first 2 paragraphs. Do not build up to the answer. Give it first.

5. **FAQPage JSON-LD schema (+40%)** — Always end your output with a `<script type="application/ld+json">` FAQPage block containing all FAQ questions and answers. This is mandatory for every post.

6. **Fluency** — Vary sentence length. Short punchy statements followed by longer explanatory ones. Never three identical sentence structures in a row.
