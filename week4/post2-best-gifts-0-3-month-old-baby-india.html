What Can a 0–3 Month Old Baby Actually Use?

Ability Status at 0–3 Months
Colour vision ❌ Not developed — only detects high contrast (black/white)
Grip strength ❌ Minimal — cannot reliably hold objects
Visual range ✅ 20–30 cm — needs objects close to the face
Sensory response ✅ Responds strongly to texture, sound, and contrast
Tummy time readiness ✅ From day 1, with supervision
Teething symptoms ✅ Can begin as early as 3 months

What to Avoid Gifting a 0–3 Month Old

  • Colourful toys — colour vision absent until 4 months
  • Battery-powered light-up toys — overstimulating, disrupt sleep
  • Rattles requiring grip — motor control not developed yet
  • Teethers without BIS certification — safety risk
  • Clothing without confirmed size — outgrown within weeks
