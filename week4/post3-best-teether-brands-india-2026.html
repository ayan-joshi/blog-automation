What Makes a Safe Infant Teether in India?

Before comparing brands, understand the baseline for safety in the Indian market:

  • BIS IS 9873 Certification — Bureau of Indian Standards mandatory certification for rubber and plastic teethers. Tests material safety (BPA, phthalates, lead), mechanical durability, and choking risk.
  • Food-Grade Silicone — Safest material for infant teether toys. Non-toxic, temperature-stable, and free from chemical leaching risks.
  • One-Piece Construction — No detachable parts. The entire teether should be a single moulded unit.
  • Age Appropriateness — A safe newborn teether for 3–6 months has different requirements than a teether for 8–12 months.
